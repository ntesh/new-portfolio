My Story

Born in North Carolina and living in Florida, I am a web developer and UI designer with over 10 years of experience. I have been doing freelance web design and graphic design since the age of 13, utilizing platforms such as Fiverr and Etsy. I started my career journey in 2017 at a local print shop. I learned the importance of brand integrity, print/design on demand, and working one on one with clients using programs such as Adobe and WordPress. In 2019, I started a new digital marketing position at a large insurance corporation. This job allowed me to fully utilize all my skills including web design, graphic design and other multimedia production through the use of technologies such as HTML5/CSS, Bootstrap4, JavaScript, WordPress, Adobe, and Figma. At the beginning of 2021, I started a new Web Developer position for a large digital marketing agency where I utilized a full-scope of technologies such as HTML5 / CSS, Bootstrap 4/5, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, Adobe, and Figma.
