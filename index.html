My Story

Born in North Carolina and living in Florida, I am a graphic designer and web developer with over 10 years of experience. I have been doing freelance graphic design since the age of 13, utilizing platforms such as Etsy and Fiverr. I started my career journey in 2017 at a local shirt printer. I learned the importance of customer service and print/design on demand. At the beginning of 2019, I started a new digital marketing position at a large insurance corporation. This job has allowed me to fully utilize all my skills including graphic design, web design, and other multimedia production. Since I have started, I have further developed my design skills and have gained exposure to a wide variety of projects and additional skills.
